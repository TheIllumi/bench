## Versioning Policy

Bench follows Semantic Versioning.

MAJOR

Breaking architectural or product changes.

Example:

v2.0.0

---

MINOR

New features.

Example:

v1.3.0

---

PATCH

Bug fixes.

Example:

v1.3.2
